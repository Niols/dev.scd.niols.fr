Niols’s Collection of (Mostly Unpublished) Scottish Country Dances

The Wee Journey

32-bar reel for six couples in a six-couple longwise set

Get the description of the dance as PDF.

On second chord, 4th, 5th and 6th couples cross.
1–8
All couples dance twelve hands round to the left for 6 bars. 5th couple in second place and 2nd couple in fifth place cross, finishing in order 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 with 6th, 4th and 2nd couples on own sides.
9–16
All dance reels of six on the sidelines for 6 bars, giving right shoulder to being. All turn by the right halfway to finish into Allemande hold, 1st, 3rd and 5th couples facing up and 2nd, 4th and 6th couples facing down.
17–24
1st, 2nd and 3rd couples at the top and 6th, 5th and 4th couples at the bottom dance interlocking Allemandes, finishing in order 3, 2, 1, 6, 5, 4 with 3rd, 1st and 5th couples on own sides.
25–32
All set. 2nd couple cross up while 3rd couple steps down; 5th couple cross down while 4th couple steps up; 1st and 6th couples dance half right hands across. All turn by the right and finish in order 2, 3, 6, 1, 4, 5.
Repeat from new positions.
Devised by Nicolas “Niols” Jeannerod in July 2022 as a gift for Rachel Shankland.
