Niols’s Collection of (Mostly Unpublished) Scottish Country Dances

Spot on

32-bar reel for any number of couples in a round-the-room formation. All face in, each man has his partner on his right

Get the description of the dance as PDF.

1–4
All advance and retire.
5–8
All advance and men retire while women stay in the middle, pulling back right shoulder to face their partner. As such, women form an inner circle facing out while men form an outer circle facing in.
9–12
Women dance hands round to the left in the inner circle while men dance hands round to the left in the outer circle. All finish facing new partners.
13–16
All turn by the right one and a half to finish with men in the centre facing out and women on the outside facing in.
17–20
Women dance hands round to the right in the outer circle while men dance hands round to the right in the inner circle. All finish facing original partners.
21–24
All dance back-to-back.
25–28
Women dance back-to-back with the man to the left of their partner.
29–32
All take promenade hold and dance round the circle anti-clockwise.
Repeat from new positions.
Devised by Shona Holmes in July 2022. Our lovely Unit 2/3 teacher Rachel Shankland started every day with a fun warm up dance and often described our better moments in dancing and teaching as “Spot on”. Thus, this easy warm up dance was born; the circles in bars 9-12 and 17-20 create perfect spots. Thank you Rachel!
