Niols’s Collection of (Mostly Unpublished) Scottish Country Dances

Emmy's Wedding

40-bar reel for four couples in a four-couple longwise set

Get the description of the dance as PDF.

1–8
»Luckenbooth Brooch«: 1st and 4th couples cast in four bars. 2nd couple step up and 3rd couple step down on bars 3-4. 1st and 4th couples dance a half figure of eight around 2nd and 3rd couples.
9–16
»Wedding Rings«: 1st and 4th couples in second and third places dance set and rotate.
17–24
»Balade dans les bois de Ronquerolles«:
17–20
1st couple turn both hands one and a half times in four bars.
21–24
4th couple cross down below 1st couple while 3rd couple cast up to second place.
25–32
»Love in Paris«: 1st couple dance figure of love around the 3rd and 4th couples and return to own sides, finishing facing out.
33–40
»Celebration at Fingask«: 1st, 3rd and 4th couples dance a promenade chain progression, finishing in order 2, 4, 1, 3.
Repeat from new positions.
Devised by Emmy Wilkinson and Matthew Stuart Dunn %at-date%.
Music by Nicolas “Niols” Jeannerod.
